COMP20008 - Data Applications: Exam Notes

Data and Food

Data analytics plays a crucial role in food analysis:

  • Food Analytics: Analyzing food samples to identify ingredients, detect contamination, or uncover unexpected elements.
  • Health Inference: Using data to infer changes in the health of the population based on food trends and consumption.
  • Reference: Evolution of Food and Drink in Ireland.

Data and Science

Data is extensively used in scientific research:

  • CERN and Large Hadron Collider: Generates about 1000 terabytes of data per second, showcasing the scale of data used in particle physics research.

Data and Sport

Data plays a significant role in sports analytics:

  • Video Analysis: Used to study player movements, strategies, and performance.
  • Wearables and GPS Tracking: Devices like GPS trackers and heart rate monitors help analyze physical performance.
  • Sensors: Skin patches behind the ear and mouthguard sensors provide data on athletes' health and impact during gameplay.

Data and Government

Data is crucial for government transparency and urban research:

  • Open Data Platforms: Government data portals like Data Victoria and Melbourne Data provide public access to a wide range of datasets.
  • AURIN: The Australian Urban Research Infrastructure Network (AURIN) provides data to support urban research and planning.
